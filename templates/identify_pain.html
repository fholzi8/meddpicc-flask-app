Identify Pain

Identifying Pain involves recognizing and understanding the customer's challenges, problems, and unmet needs that your solution can address.

Importance of Identifying Pain

  • Allows you to tailor your solution to the customer's specific needs
  • Helps create urgency and motivation to make a change
  • Enables you to articulate the value of your solution more effectively

Strategies for Identifying Pain

  1. Ask probing questions during discovery calls
  2. Research industry trends and common challenges
  3. Analyze the customer's current processes and systems
  4. Listen for pain points mentioned in conversations
