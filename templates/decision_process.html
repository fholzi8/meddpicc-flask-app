Decision Process

The Decision Process refers to the steps and procedures a company follows to make a purchasing decision.

Importance of Understanding the Decision Process

  • Helps you navigate the sales cycle more effectively
  • Allows you to anticipate and prepare for each stage
  • Enables you to identify and address potential roadblocks

Common Stages in a Decision Process

  1. Problem identification
  2. Information gathering
  3. Evaluation of alternatives
  4. Purchase decision
  5. Post-purchase evaluation
