Paper Process

The Paper Process refers to the administrative and legal steps required to finalize a deal, including contract negotiations and approvals.

Why the Paper Process Matters

  • Ensures all legal and administrative requirements are met
  • Helps anticipate and prevent delays in closing the deal
  • Allows for better planning of resources and timelines

Key Elements of the Paper Process

  1. Contract drafting and review
  2. Legal approval
  3. Procurement process
  4. Signature and execution
  5. Payment terms and invoicing
