Champion

A Champion is an internal advocate within the customer's organization who supports your solution and helps drive the deal forward.

The Role of a Champion

  • Provides insider information about the decision-making process
  • Advocates for your solution to key stakeholders
  • Helps navigate internal politics and overcome objections

Strategies for Developing and Leveraging a Champion

  1. Identify potential champions early in the sales process
  2. Provide them with compelling information and resources
  3. Support their efforts to promote your solution internally
  4. Maintain regular communication and offer assistance
