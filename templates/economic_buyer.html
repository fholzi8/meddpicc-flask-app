Economic Buyer

The Economic Buyer is the key decision-maker who controls the budget and has the authority to make the final purchasing decision.

Importance of Identifying the Economic Buyer

  • Ensures you're targeting the right person with decision-making power
  • Helps tailor your pitch to address their specific concerns and priorities
  • Increases the likelihood of closing the deal

Strategies to Identify and Engage

  1. Research the company's organizational structure
  2. Ask your main contact about the decision-making process
  3. Look for individuals with titles like CEO, CFO, or department heads
  4. Prepare materials that address high-level business impact
