Decision Criteria

Decision Criteria are the factors and requirements that the customer will use to evaluate and choose between different solutions or vendors.

Why Understanding Decision Criteria is Crucial

  • Allows you to align your proposal with the customer's needs
  • Helps you differentiate your solution from competitors
  • Increases your chances of being selected as the preferred vendor

Types of Decision Criteria

  1. Technical requirements
  2. Financial considerations (cost, ROI)
  3. Implementation timeline
  4. Support and maintenance
  5. Vendor reputation and stability
