Metrics

Metrics are key performance indicators (KPIs) that measure the success of a deal. They help quantify the value proposition and ROI for the customer.

Why Metrics Matter

  • Provide concrete evidence of potential value
  • Help align the solution with customer's business objectives
  • Support decision-making process with data-driven insights

Key Considerations

  1. Identify relevant metrics for the customer's industry and specific situation
  2. Gather baseline metrics to show current state
  3. Project improvements based on your solution
  4. Use metrics to build a compelling business case
